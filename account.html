FAQs

General Questions

What is Native Aura?
Native Aura is an online platform that connects customers with authentic, handcrafted products made by tribal communities of Karnataka. We aim to preserve and promote their rich cultural heritage.

How does shopping on Native Aura support tribal artisans?
Every purchase directly benefits tribal artisans by providing them with a sustainable livelihood and a platform to showcase their unique crafts.

Orders and Shipping

How do I track my order?
Once your order is placed, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also log in to your account and navigate to the "Orders" section to track your order.

What is the estimated delivery time?
Our delivery time ranges from 5-10 business days, depending on your location and the product's availability.

Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we deliver only within India. We are working on expanding our services to other countries.

What are the shipping charges?
Shipping is free for orders above ₹1000. For orders below ₹1000, a nominal fee of ₹50-100 applies, depending on the delivery location.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?
We accept returns for damaged or defective items within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in its original packaging.

How do I initiate a return or refund?
To request a return, log in to your account, go to "Orders," select the product, and click "Request Return." Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the product is returned.

Are all items eligible for return?
Customized or made-to-order products are non-returnable. However, if you receive a damaged item, we will replace it or issue a refund.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major payment methods, including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

Is COD available for all products?
Yes, COD is available for all orders below ₹5000. For orders above ₹5000, we require online payment.

Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use secure payment gateways with end-to-end encryption to ensure your payment details are safe.

Products

Are the products authentic?
Yes, all products on Native Aura are handcrafted by tribal artisans and sourced directly from them to ensure authenticity.

Can I customize the products?
Some artisans offer customization. Check the product description or contact customer support for more details.

What types of products are available on Native Aura?
We offer a wide range of products, including:

  • Handcrafted jewelry
  • Traditional textiles
  • Home décor
  • Organic produce
  • Art and collectibles

About Us

Welcome to Native Aura! Native Aura is a dedicated platform aimed at showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Karnataka's tribal communities. Our goal is to connect users with the vibrant traditions, crafts, and stories of these lesser-known tribes, fostering appreciation and support for their unique contributions.

Mission: Preserve and promote the diverse cultural heritage of Karnataka's tribal communities while providing a platform for their unique products.

Vision: Become a leading resource for promoting tribal art and culture across Karnataka and beyond, building awareness and appreciation for indigenous communities.

Objectives: Educate users, support local artisans, and engage users with interactive features.
